### LIMA license information

License information of the LIMA dataset:
If the source data of LIMA has a stricter license than CC BY-NC-SA, the LIMA dataset follows the same. Otherwise, it follows the CC BY-NC-SA license.

Source of data: https://huggingface.co/datasets/GAIR/lima

File `lima.en.jsonl' contains examples from the original LIMA dataset with changed formatting.
